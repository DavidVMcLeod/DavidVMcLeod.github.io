Research Group

I am currently looking for Masters and/or PhD level students interested in evolutionary theory. These fully-funded positions will start in January 2025 or September 2025. Students will be hosted in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the Université de Montréal. Although Université de Montréal is a french-language institute at the undergraduate level, graduate studies can be completed in French or English.

I currently do not have funding for postdocs. Postdocs with external funding (e.g., NSERC, FRQ, or CRM fellowships) are welcome to contact me at any time.
